TEAM SPECIFICATION

A team may consist of (a maximum of) 4 participants.

TASK

Teams must build a manual bot capable completing an entire track consisting of different terrain within a fixed time limit, must have a hand/gripping mechanism.

ARENA

The arena will be completely automated in itself. All the task and timing will be computer generated Entire arena is divided into several zones each having a different challenge to overcome. It is a one on one event where 2 bots will compete at the same time.

3-D model of the arena is given below.

For more views of the arena click here and download the images.

RULES

  • There will be 3 rounds.
  • A maximum of 5 minutes will be given to traverse the arena.
  • A maximum of 3 restarts will be given to a team.
  • No penalty will be awarded for a restart.
  • During a restart, the bot will have to be restarted by putting it back on the previous checkpoint.
  • In a restart, the timer will not be set back to zero.
  • Any damage to the arena by the bot will lead to immediate disqualification of the team.

    • ROUND 1:

    This is the round of Elimination .It is a one on one event where 2 bots will compete at the same time . In Zone 1 the rotating rollers will rotate in a reverse direction so as to oppose the bot to move forward through the marbles and then through a reverse rotating conveyer belt. In Zone 2 the bot has to pick/grip the cubes and place them in the path holes in the track . The cubes shall be placed at their respective positions of the arena which has to be traversed through a rotating disk at the centre. In Zone 3 , a switch will be present , on pressing the switch the bridge will become functional.

    ROUND 2

    It is a one on one event where 2 bots will compete at the same time. In Zone 1 the rollers and the conveyers will move faster than round 1. Zone 3 is same as zone 2 and the bridge will become functional after placing all the cubes in their respective places and pressing the switch. The bot, after completing the task will have to reach to the finishing podium.

    ROUND 3

    In Zone 1&2 the rollers, the conveyer and rotating disc will move faster than that in Round 2. Zone 3 is same as zone 2 and the bridge shall remain functional after placing all the cubes and pressing the switch. The bot, after completing the task will have to reach the finishing podium.

    BOT SPECIFICATION

    The top view projection of the bot must fit within a square of dimensions 250mm x 250mm (l x b)

    POWER SUPPLY The bots may use on-board power supply. External AC power supply (230V, 50Hz) will be provided. Each team shall bring their own power supply for its bots. The potential difference between any two points on the bots must not exceed 24 V DC .

    SCORING:

    To be updated on the website a week before the event.

Movimento

PROBLEM STATEMENT:

Build a manual robot with hand mechanism and an autonomous robot which can follow black line in white surface.

More » Less »

ARENA:

A sample arena is given below.

  • There will be two zones, red zone for manual robot and rest is for autonomous robot.
  • There will be 4 checkpoints as marked by red flags.
  • The width of black line will be 2cm.
  • The gates will be of white color , 11cm of height and placed 2.5cm above the ground.

3-D model is given below.

Gates :

GAMEPLAY:

  • There will be two rounds.
  • In first round only autonomous robot has to traverse from START 1 to 4th checkpoint.
  • No sensors or gates will be activated during first round of play.
  • In second round all the sensors and gates will be activated.
  • The GATE 1 for the manual robot will be opened only after the autonomous robot crosses the SENSOR 1.
  • Then the manual robot has to start from START 2 and pick the box(5cm*5cm*5cm) and place it on the SENSOR 2.
  • GATE 2 will be opened for the autonomous robot only after placing the box on the SENSOR 2.
  • Similarly GATE 3 for autonomous robot will be opened only after the autonomous robot crosses SENSOR 3.
  • The autonomous robot has to wait in front of the gate until the gate is closed.
  • GATE 3 is a feedback path, so it is to be avoided.

RULES:

  • A maximum no of 4 participants are allowed in a team.
  • The team will be provided 220V, 50Hz standard AC supply.
  • Participants should bring any other power supply required for their robots.
  • Teams qualifying 1st round will go into the final round.
  • Lego kits or any pre-made spare parts are not allowed.
  • Teams cannot touch their robots during run.
  • In 1st round 3 runs will be given to each team and best score among those will be considered.
  • Maximum 3 restarts will be available for each run. No penalty will be given for the restarts but the time will not be set to zero.
  • The decision of the coordinator will be the final decision.

BOT SPECIFICATION:

  • The manual BOT should fix in a box of 25cm*25cm(l*b) or less, no constraint on height.
  • The manual BOT can be wired or wireless, can have on-board or off-board power supply.
  • The autonomous BOT should fix in a box of 20cm*20cm(l*b) or less, no constraint on height.
  • The autonomous BOT must have on board power supply.

SCORING:

  • Each checkpoint = 100 points.
  • In 2nd round putting the box on SENSOR 2 will be of 50 points and crossing the finish line will be of 150 points.
  • In both round total score = Total points-time(in seconds) taken for completing the task.

CETronics

Each team contains 2 students from any branch and year.

Elimination Round

 

This is the first part of the technical quiz competition. Based on this Elimination round, will select 8 teams for the final round. (4 teams from CET and 4 from others) Elimination Round contains 30 questions based on basic electronics and technology. Participants will get only 30 minutes to solve this. There will be no options for the questions and NO negatives marks. If TIE between teams, then the STAR marked questions will be considered.

More » Less »

FINAL PART

Total 8 teams will be selected for this part. There are 4 different rounds of this part.

Round 01 : Basic electronics Round

  • Questions of this round based on the Basic electronics.
  • Each team will get 2 questions
  • Each questions carries 10 marks
  • If one team not answered, that question will pass to next team. Passed question carries 5 marks.

    • Round 02 : Photo Identification Round

  • Participants wanted to identify the person/instruments shown in the screen
  • Each team will get 2 questions
  • Each question carries 10 marks.
  • If one team not answered, that question will pass to next team. Passed question carries 5 marks.

    • Round 03 : Latest Technology Round

  • In this round, participants will get general knowledge questions.
  • Each team will get 2 questions
  • Each question carries 10 marks.
  • If one team not answered, that question will pass to next team. Passed question carries 5 marks.

    • Round 04 : JACKPOT Round

  • In this round, each team will get 5 clues and they wanted to find out the answer from those clues.
  • If they answered in first clue, they will get 25 marks. If they take more clues to answer means points will decrease 5 marks respectively.
  • Each team will get one question only. No passing

    • Round 05 : Rapid fire

  • In this round, participants can choose their favorite subjects from Quiz master’s different options like electronics, mechatronics, Computer, electrical, biomedical etc.
  • Each team will get 10 questions, each questions carries 10 marks.
  • For each team will get only 2 minutes to answer 10 questions.
  • If one team not answered, that question will NOT pass to next team.

    • At the end of these rounds, TIE between top two team means, will ask more question to each team to find out winner.
